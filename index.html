How booking works

Start with dates, passenger count, luggage and preferred airports. Brokers provide quotes, you select an option, sign the trip sheet or contract, and pay per operator terms. Confirm manifests and special requests 24–72 hours before departure.

What flying private is like

Skip long lines, use smaller airports, enjoy personalized onboard service, and faster door-to-door time. Expect premium catering, privacy, and flexible routing.

Pricing & instant quotes

Quotes combine hourly rates, estimated block time, and known fees (FBO handling, taxes, positioning). Always request an itemized breakdown showing flight time, repositioning, handling, catering, and surcharges.

Typical booking procedure

  1. Request or search with dates, pax, luggage, airports.
  2. Receive quotes and aircraft options.
  3. Select aircraft and confirm price.
  4. Sign contract and pay deposit/full amount.
  5. Prepare manifest, catering, ground transport.
  6. Flight day: FBO access and expedited boarding.
