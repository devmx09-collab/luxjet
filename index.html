Private luxury jet booking process

Start with dates, passenger count, luggage and preferred airports. Brokers provide quotes, you select an option, sign the trip sheet or contract, and pay per operator terms. Confirm manifests and special requests 24–72 hours before departure.

What flying private is like

Skip long lines, private luxury jets use smaller airports, enjoy personalized onboard service, and faster door-to-door time. Expect premium catering, privacy, and flexible routing.

Private luxury jet pricing & instant quotes

Quotes for private luxury jets combine hourly rates, estimated block time, and known fees (FBO handling, taxes, positioning). Always request an itemized breakdown showing flight time, repositioning, handling, catering, and surcharges.

Quick tips for first timers

  • Check baggage rules for your aircraft class.
  • Share dietary, pet, mobility, and pickup details early.
  • Request operator credentials (AOC, insurance, audits) if you need reassurance.
  • Be punctual; follow the operator's arrival window.

If you've never flown on a private jet — What to expect step‑by‑step

A concise walkthrough of a typical private jet trip from booking to arrival

1. Inquiry & quoting

Provide dates, airports, passenger count, luggage, and special needs (pets, catering, accessibility).

2. Compare & confirm

Review itemized pricing (flight time, positioning, handling, taxes, catering). Confirm aircraft, pick-up/drop-off windows, sign the trip sheet/contract, and pay deposit or full amount as required.

3. Pre-flight coordination

Send passenger manifests and ID details, finalize catering, and request ground transport. Confirm the FBO terminal and arrival window.

4. Day-of logistics

Arrive during the specified window at the FBO for expedited check-in. Crew completes briefings, weight/balance checks, and loading before boarding.

5. Onboard experience

Enjoy personalized service, seating configuration, catering, and Wi‑Fi (varies). Crew will cover safety and comfort items.

6. Arrival & ground transport

Arrivals are quick—customs/immigration may be handled at the FBO for private international flights. Pre-book ground transport or valet to minimize waiting.

Practical checklist

  • Valid government ID and passports (if international).
  • Confirm carry‑on and checked baggage limits with operator.
  • Notify dietary, medical, or pet needs in advance.
  • Confirm pickup time and FBO terminal location.
  • Save operator contact and trip reference on your phone.
